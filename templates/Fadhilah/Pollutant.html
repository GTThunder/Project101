Pollutant from Vehicles

Transportation is a single source of air pollution. There are many pollutants that add up to the percentage of air pollution. Major pollutants are Particulate matter (PM), Hydrocarbons (HC), Nitrogen oxides (NOx), Carbon monoxide (CO),Sulfur dioxide (SO2), Toxics and Greenhouse Gases. All this contributes to the increasing PSI readings in Singapore.
